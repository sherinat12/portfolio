Why I build

I'm a final-year Computer Science Engineering student at Francis Xavier Engineering College, Tirunelveli, graduating in 2027 with a current CGPA of 8.0. My interest in software started with a simple question I ask about most things around me: "why isn't this easier to use?"

That question has taken me from building a responsive ordering site for a local-style pet supply store, to designing a personal portfolio system, to building a machine-learning-assisted tool that flags suspicious URLs before someone clicks them.
